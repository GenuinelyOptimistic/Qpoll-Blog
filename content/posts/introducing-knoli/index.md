We've been working on Knoli quietly for a while now, and today we're excited to share what we've built with a broader audience.

## The problem we're solving

Every day, businesses spend billions of dollars trying to understand what people think. Market research firms, survey companies, focus groups — the data economy is enormous. Yet the people whose opinions power all of that research? They get nothing.

We think that's backwards.

## What Knoli is

Knoli is a mobile-first polling platform that pays you for every poll you respond to. It's simple:

- Someone posts a poll question
- You answer honestly
- You earn real money

No lengthy surveys. No waiting weeks for a gift card that may or may not arrive. Just quick, honest questions and fast payouts.

## Why we built it this way

We wanted to make the experience as friction-free as possible. Creating a poll takes seconds. Voting is one tap. Results are live the moment the poll closes.

We also care deeply about the quality of responses. When people are paid fairly, they answer honestly. And honest data is worth more to everyone.

## What's next

We're starting with a small beta. If you want in, request access on [knoli.app](https://knoli.app). We're rolling out invites every week.

We'll be writing here regularly — product updates, what we're learning, and ideas about how crowd-sourced opinions can shape better decisions. Stay tuned.
